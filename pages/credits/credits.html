The Second Realm is a project carried by The Bitcoin Museum and now deployed as a public installation within a dedicated space at Bitcoin House Bali.

The Bitcoin Museum

The Bitcoin Museum is a Bitcoin-only, open-source educational and cultural initiative dedicated to protecting the integrity of the Bitcoin protocol through three core missions: education, preservation, and open knowledge sharing.

The distributed autonomous consensus, known as the Nakamoto Consensus, stands as one of the most important innovations in human history, and the institution exists to ensure it endures. Follow on X for more information.

Bitcoin House Bali

Bitcoin House Bali is a non-profit initiative that aims to use Bitcoin as a tool to support human rights and financial sovereignty in Indonesia. They provide free and open-source education.

You can explore hidden treasures in Bitcoin blockchain data on the second day of every month at Canggu, Bali.
